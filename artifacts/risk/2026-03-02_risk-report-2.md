## Model Assumptions and Limitations

1. **Beta estimates use 5-year monthly data from public sources.** Estimation error of +/- 0.2 to 0.3 typical. Several betas were revised in the prior cycle (VRT from 1.55 to 2.07, COHR from 1.68 to 1.87, MRVL from 1.97 to 2.26, VST from 1.63 to 1.42).

2. **Correlation estimates are structural, not statistical.** In stress events, within-cluster correlations converge toward 1.0, making VaR estimates potentially understated during drawdowns. The NVIDIA/COHR partnership likely increases COHR-NVDA structural correlation going forward.

3. **VIX-adjusted VaR uses proportional scaling.** The relationship between VIX and individual stock volatilities is approximate. VIX close estimate of ~22 used; the actual close may differ.

4. **Parametric VaR does not capture fat tails, gap risk, or discrete earnings events.** The March 4-5 earnings cluster plus tariff plus geopolitical escalation is a non-normal risk. True 99th percentile losses may be 25-35% higher than parametric estimates.

5. **COHR price uses the post-announcement ~$283 level.** This is based on extended trading data. The regular session close may differ if the announcement came after hours. The PM should verify the opening price on March 3.

6. **Geopolitical scenario estimates are highly uncertain.** Iran escalation pathways have wide confidence intervals. The stress test estimates above are illustrative, not precise.

7. **LRCX beta estimated at ~1.70 based on semi-equipment sector comps.** Actual beta may vary. This estimate is used for the conditional replacement assessment only.
