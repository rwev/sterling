# Sterling — Portfolio Update

## What Changed

We closed our speculative nuclear small-cap position (3% of NAV) and deployed the capital into **Frontline (FRO)**, the world's largest independent VLCC operator, as a targeted hedge against the Iran escalation scenario now unfolding. Tanker spot rates have surged to an all-time high of $423,736 per day as supply route disruptions intensify. Our risk analytics confirm this is the most risk-efficient addition to the portfolio in our fund's history — it has negative correlation with eight of our nine existing positions and nearly halves the marginal risk contribution relative to its standalone volatility.

The nuclear position exited cleanly; the litigation overhang lifts. We retain nuclear exposure through our substantial positions in power generation (Constellation and Vistra), where the thesis remains constructive and catalyst-driven.
