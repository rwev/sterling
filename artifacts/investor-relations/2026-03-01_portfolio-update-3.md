## Where We Stand

The portfolio is overweight AI infrastructure and power generation—the two secular tailwinds we expect to drive returns this decade. Semiconductors are exactly at our 25% sector limit (Broadcom 13%, NVIDIA 10%, Marvell 2%). Power utilities sit at 20% (Vistra dominant). Data center infrastructure (cooling and service) is 12%. IT services and hardware round out the position.

Cash has grown from 9% to 11% on a target basis (23% deployed, reflecting our phased-entry positioning). This buffer is intentional. Elevated volatility and March's event risk warrant flexibility.

The portfolio is concentrated—10 positions, fully invested thesis except for tranche capacity. That concentration is not a flaw; it's the structure that generates alpha. We're not chasing breadth; we're compounding conviction.
