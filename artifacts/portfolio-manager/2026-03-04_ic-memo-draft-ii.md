**HOWEVER**: The portfolio is full at 10/10. LNG must be evaluated against the weakest existing holdings for displacement.

**Displacement Analysis**: The weakest R/R positions in the portfolio are VRT (1.2:1), AEM (1.5:1), and IPGP (1.3:1). LNG at 2.1:1 is superior to all three on R/R.

**LNG also carries elevated binary risk**: A ceasefire/Hormuz reopening could unwind the LNG premium rapidly. The analyst notes this in Risk Factor #1. The thesis quality is high but the catalyst is geopolitically dependent, similar to FRO. Adding a second geopolitical-dependent energy position (alongside FRO at 7%) would increase the portfolio's ceasefire risk materially.

**Decision: CONDITIONALLY APPROVED.** LNG is a high-quality thesis with 2.1:1 R/R, but the portfolio is full with no clean displacement candidate.
